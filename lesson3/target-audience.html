Target Audience:

The target audience for the Chamber of Commerce Site is local business owners and entrepreneurs. They are looking for information and resources to help them start or grow their businesses, as well as opportunities to network and connect with other businesses in the area.

Personas:

"Samantha": Samantha is a 35-year-old small business owner who runs a boutique clothing store in the downtown area. She is always looking for ways to attract more customers and increase sales.


"Mike": Mike is a 40-year-old entrepreneur who is in the process of starting a new business in the area. He is looking for information and resources to help him get his business off the ground and make connections with other local business owners.

Scenarios:

1. Samantha visits the Chamber of Commerce Site to find out about upcoming networking events where she can connect with other local business owners.

2. Mike visits the site to find information on business resources such as funding opportunities and business planning tools.

3. A new business owner visits the site to find out about local business regulations and requirements.

4. A resident visits the site to find out about local businesses in the area and to support them.

5. A business owner visits the site to find out about local business services such as marketing and web design.
