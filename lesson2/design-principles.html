Design Principles

Wilson Jr Manzon

Alignment

Brigham Young University

byu.edu

All of the elements are left-aligned to to make reading easier for the user. Aligning the elements also makes the website look neat and organized.

Hick's Law

Tesla, Inc.

tesla.com

Tesla's website applies the Hick's law by giving the user straight-forward call-to-actions. There are no flowery words that take the customer's attention away from the CTAs.

Contrast

Netflix

netflix.com

Having a contrast of white text and black background not only gives clarity and eye comfort, but also gives a feel of the cinema. Not only that! It also makes the colored movies stand out.
