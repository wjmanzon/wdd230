Discover Rexburg

Rexburg is a small city located in Madison County, Idaho, with a population of approximately 29,000 people as of 2021. It is home to Brigham Young University-Idaho, a private university affiliated with The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
The city has a thriving student community, with a large percentage of the population made up of college students. As a result, there are many affordable housing options, restaurants, and entertainment venues that cater to students.
Rexburg is surrounded by stunning natural beauty, with many outdoor recreational opportunities, including skiing, snowmobiling, hiking, and fishing. Some popular tourist spots in the area include Yellowstone National Park, Grand Teton National Park, and the St. Anthony Sand Dunes.
The city is also home to the Legacy Flight Museum, which features a collection of vintage aircraft from World War II and the Korean War. The museum is open to the public and offers guided tours and educational programs.
Additionally, the Rexburg Idaho Temple, a beautiful Latter-day Saint temple, is a popular destination for visitors and locals alike.
